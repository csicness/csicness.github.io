About Me

My name is Curtis and I like to create things...

My work is made up of Illustration, Concept-Art, and Web Design. I have a B.A in Printmaking from Rhode Island College.

My work is very simplistic. While working toward my degree, I focused on woodblock printing. This type of art forced me to think in a minimalistic way.
